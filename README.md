## Project Overview
This repository contains my complete Digital Literacy Portfolio, submitted as the final internal project for the CSE0001 - Digital Literacy course. As a Student Digital Ambassador, this portfolio serves as a comprehensive guide for peers to navigate the digital world safely, professionally, and efficiently. It includes visual resources, professional profiles, collaboration tools, communication guidelines, and cybercrime awareness materials.

## Module Summaries

* **Module 1: Digital Literacy Awareness** Contains a visual infographic designed to educate batchmates on the definition of digital literacy, essential digital tools for students, and safe internet practices.
* **Module 2: Student Digital Portfolio**
  Showcases the establishment of a professional online presence through active profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Stack Overflow. 
* **Module 3: Coding & Collaboration Platforms**
  Features a completed beginner-level Python challenge on HackerRank and a collaborative cloud-based assessment tool. 
  * **Interactive Quiz:** [Take the Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz Here](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfOTXSvEykSYiP6nSDiQmlmrzryoMM6x8kHDlqFNXTG7XLUaA/viewform?usp=publish-editor)
* **Module 4: Professional Email & Etiquette Guide**
  Includes drafted professional emails for academic extensions and internship inquiries, alongside a 10-point 'Do's and Don'ts' checklist for responsible social media usage.
* **Module 5: Cybercrime Awareness & Prevention**
  Presents a case study on the "Fake Customer Support" UPI Scam, detailing the mechanics of the fraud, targeted demographics, and an 8-point prevention checklist for college students in India.
