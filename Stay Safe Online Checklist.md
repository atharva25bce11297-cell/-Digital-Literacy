# **Stay Safe Online Checklist**



### 

### **Prevention Guide for College Students:**



* ##### The Golden UPI Rule: You NEVER need to enter your UPI PIN to receive money. A PIN is only used to send money or check your balance.

##### 

* ##### Verify UPI IDs: Always double-check the receiver's name and UPI ID before hitting send, especially if paying a new merchant near campus.

##### 

* ##### Use Official Support: Never search for customer care numbers on Google. Only use the official help section inside the verified app (like Swiggy, Zomato, or Amazon).

##### 

* ##### Beware of Urgent Demands: Scammers create a false sense of urgency (e.g., "Your account will be blocked in 10 minutes"). Pause and verify before acting.

##### 

* ##### Enable 2FA: Turn on Two-Factor Authentication for all your important accounts, especially your college email and primary social media.

##### 

* ##### Avoid Public Wi-Fi for Banking: Never make financial transactions or check bank balances while connected to open, unsecured college or cafe Wi-Fi networks.

##### 

* ##### Strong, Unique Passwords: Do not use the same password for your university portal and your personal bank account.

##### 

* ##### Think Before You Click: Do not click on links in unsolicited SMS messages or WhatsApp forwards claiming you have won a prize or received a package.



#### &#x20;**Emergency Reporting Channels:**



* ##### **If you or a friend are victimized by cybercrime, report it immediately to the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call the Cyber Crime Helpline at 1930.**
